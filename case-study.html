How the Research Agent Works

The research pipeline combines high-throughput single-pass LLM extraction with a rigorous multi-stage verification and deterministic calibration loop. All final opportunity tiers are computed by an immutable deterministic state machine (src/engine/verdict.ts).

PHASE 1

Architecture Foundation & Immutable Benchmark

100 APPS PROCESSED

Established the canonical Zod schema, versioned JSON storage, and immutable 100-app benchmark dictionary from data/benchmark_100.json.

PHASE 2

Gate B Deep-Verified Calibration

10 APPS PROCESSED

Executed comprehensive 4-stage deep verification (Discovery -> Extraction -> Evidence Validation -> Critic Audit) across 10 stratified sample applications.

PHASE 3

Fast Scale One-Pass Production Execution

90 APPS PROCESSED

Executed high-throughput single-pass extraction across the remaining 90 applications with deterministic evidence validation and conservative risk guardrails.

PHASE 4

Independent Stratified Verification Audit

15 APPS PROCESSED

Independently audited a 15-app stratified sample across both modes, confirming 97.5% post-verification accuracy and diagnosing conservative guardrails.

PHASE 5

Final Dataset Calibration & Provenance Audit

100 APPS PROCESSED

Applied zero-LLM local deterministic recalibration + 15 targeted LLM verification checks on borderline apps, establishing strict 4-tier provenance.

Methodology & Provenance Tier Definitions (Strict 4-Tier Separation)
independently_verified
Applications verified during our independent 15-app stratified audit (data/verification/app_*_verified.json) using fresh official sources crawled via FastCheerioFetcherProvider and evaluated by gemini-2.5-flash without altering baseline records.
targeted_verified
High-value borderline applications verified during Part 4 targeted verification (data/calibrated/targeted_verified/app_*_targeted.json) where specific unresolved material fields were re-audited via a dedicated LLM call with fresh documentation.
deterministically_calibrated
Applications whose buildability verdicts changed cleanly via local deterministic rules (Part 2) where raw baseline facts (public_api: 'yes', credentials_obtainable_without_approval: true) satisfied exact evidence-backed thresholds without requiring an additional LLM call.
baseline_unverified
Applications whose raw initial extraction facts and verdicts were retained without external LLM verification or recalibration changes (0 false human verification claims).
baseline_unverified_conservative_unclear
54 baseline-unverified conservative UNCLEAR — retained because the one-pass system did not produce enough validated evidence for confident promotion.

Honest Misses & Uncertainty Analysis

Intellectual honesty requires transparently separating what was rigorously verified from what was retained under conservative single-pass guardrails. We never label baseline unverified apps as human-verified.

The UNCLEAR Funnel & Separation of Ambiguity

70 UNCLEAR after Deterministic Recalibration  (Part 2: 3 promoted by exact raw facts + verified sample adjustments) −6 Resolved
61 Final Presentation UNCLEAR Apps  (Part 4: 15 targeted LLM re-audits on borderline apps) −9 Promoted
↳ 7 Genuinely Unresolved Apps  (Dedicated verification confirmed true multi-tenant gating / waitlists: #7 Zoho CRM, #8 Close, #12 Intercom, #14 Front, #16 LiveAgent, #18 Help Scout, #96 Devin) True Ambiguity
↳ 54 Conservative Baseline-Unverified Apps  (retained because the one-pass system did not produce enough validated evidence for confident promotion) Guardrail Retained
Key Takeaway on Uncertainty: UNCLEAR does not mean "no API exists." It means the single-pass system did not accumulate enough validated evidence to promote the verdict confidently under the system's decision rules. 5 of 7 sampled baseline UNCLEAR verdicts (71.4%) were resolved during independent verification. The remaining 54 baseline-unverified UNCLEAR apps were intentionally preserved rather than extrapolating the sample result to unverified records.
